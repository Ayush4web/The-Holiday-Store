Day 1 - Arrival in Riyadh | Day at Leisure

Welcome to Riyadh, the birthplace of modern Saudi Arabia, where old-world charm meets 21st-century vision. Riyadh translates to “the Gardens,” a reference to the area’s past that dates back to the 14th century, when the land was revered for its fertile soil, many canals and trees. Today, Riyadh Province, also known as Al Wosta, is home to the country’s capital: a modern metropolis with a thriving financial and business center and a growing cultural scene. It’s a city rich in history, boasting myriad forts, palaces and museums, and some of the country’s most colorful souqs. It’s also home to the country’s oldest university, King Saud University, plus one of the world’s tallest skyscrapers, Kingdom Centre, and more than 4,000 mosques. Upon your arrival in Riyadh. Day is free for leisure. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 2 - Riyadh City tour

After breakfast, proceed for a Riyadh city tour, We will start our tour of mystic Riyadh by visiting the National museum .The National Museum is the primary custodian of the Saudi national heritage and culture, the Museum is a place of learning and discovery, it completes the story of Arabian civilization from the creation of the universe to our modern era . We continue thereafter to the Murabba Palace that is one of the historic buildings of the city . The palace was named after its square with the form of 400 by 400 meters. It is one of the museums in the city . We will continue our drive through the city, passing the famous Kingdom tower, the Faisaliah hotel, both a great architectural achievement. We will see also many other landmarks that make the city famous today . We are at the early stages of the birth of a new nation .Lunch will served at Najd Village. evening at Leisure. Dinner at Hotel ( Overnight stay at the Hotel)

Day 3 - Riyadh - Jeddah

This day marks the end of a fabulous vacation to Riyadh. Rise to a charming morning in Riyadh and indulge in a mouth-watering breakfast at your hotel , check out from your hotel (check-out time is 1200 hours) before you head to the airport for your flight to Jeddah. Welcome to JEDDAH - Where ancient and modern meet Over the years, no Saudi city has been more open to outside influencers such as traders, international artists and Makkah-bound pilgrims than the ancient port of Jeddah. Indeed, the city’s unofficial motto is “Jeddah ghair,” or “Jeddah’s different.” Today, Jeddah is Saudi Arabia’s buzzing cosmopolitan hub, home to gleaming hotels and big-ticket events like the Red Sea International Film Festival, which takes place in late winter. The city’s heart is still intact in Al Balad, the magical historical quarter that has undergone a renaissance in recent years. And the Red Sea is still central to it all — for trade, for diving among pristine reefs, and for fishing for Jeddah’s legendary seafood. The city where Eve was laid to rest (according to local lore) is a beguiling mix of old and new, and one that remains gloriously different.. Upon your arrival in Jeddah. Day is free for leisure. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 4 - Jeddah City tour

After breakfast, proceed for a Jeddah city tour. We will start our tour by visiting the Old Jeddah.Our guide will tell you the amazing stories about Jeddah and its surroundings .We will enjoy a wonderful walk in Al-Balad , the old part of the city .This walking tour of the old Town will take you along all the less known must-see local spots: Old Market hidden gems, Old Jeddah hidden galleries, local Jeddah food only for locals… While visiting Al-Balad, visitors will be taken to see Beit Nassif, one of the prominent renovated structures in the old city, which dates back to the mid 1800's. The five-story building provides a rare opportunity for the visitors to experience the blend of traditional architecture with modernity. We will see also the Baghdadi house. We will also visit the Old gold market in Gabel street and the Ukash Mosque. We will end our tour driving towards the Jeddah Corniche which is the 30 km coastal resort area of the city of Jeddah. We will pass by the Al Rahmah Mosque. Located along the Red Sea, the corniche features a coastal road, recreation areas, pavilions and large-scale civic sculptures as well as King Fahd's Fountain, the highest fountain in the world.

Upon reaching the hotel, complete the check-in formalities and head to your room; you have the rest of the day at leisure. You can choose to spend the day resting or simply head out of your hotel and explore the streets of Dubai on your own - you will find things to see and do around.

Day 6 - Dubai Canal Cruise

After the breakfas rest of the day at leisure . In the evening Cruise the iconic canal of Dubai through the heart of Business Bay on a Dubai Water Canal Cruise! Settle into our dhow which sails you across the region’s most stylish contemporary sights in the company of finest foods and entertainment. Take our New Dubai Water Canal Cruise to enjoy Dubai’s newest attractions at its magical! Aboard a 2 Hour Catamaran Dubai Water Canal Cruise, you’ll float through the heart of Dubai, passing by Ras Al Khor Sanctuary, Sheikh Zayed Road Waterfall Bridge, Burj Khalifa, WHotel, St Regis, and JW Marriott Marquis, among other iconic and impressive sights. What makes this cruise even more exciting is that you’ll get to sail on the newest and grandest Dubai Water Canal. Featuring a captivating waterfront of more than six kilometers length, the canal stretches from Business Bay through Safa Park and Jumeirah to the Arabian Gulf, culminating at the Jumeirah Beach Park. On this 2.5 hour cruise, you’ll take in the glamorous attractions along Business Bay and Sheik Zayed Road, drift under the resplendently-lit bridges, and see locals as well as tourists walking along the banks of the canal. Besides unfolding before you Dubai’s gorgeous nighttime scenery, this high spirited experience also lets you feast on a wide assortment of local and international delights,served buffet style in an intimate setting offering an unmatched from-the-water vantage point of the city highlights.
